Ludovit "Mr. Highlight" Klein is a professional mixed martial artist from Slovakia competing in the Lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). Born on February 22, 1995, Klein has a professional record of 21 wins, 4 losses, and 1 draw. He stands 5 feet 7 inches tall and fights with a southpaw stance.

Key career statistics include:
- Significant Strikes per Minute (SLpM): 3.96
- Fighting out of: Slovakia
- Current ranking: Top 30 Lightweight MMA fighter according to Tapology and #74 Lightweight according to Fight Matrix.

Notable fights and achievements:
- Klein scored a remarkable finish at UFC Fight Night 238 with just the third finish in UFC history stemming from a front kick to the body.
- His last recorded fight before the current date was against Jai Herbert on March 18, 2023, at UFC 286 in London.
- Klein has shown a strong performance in his recent fights with a record of 3 wins, 0 losses, and 1 draw over the past four fights.

Klein is known for his striking abilities, as evidenced by his high SLpM rate, and has demonstrated significant versatility and skill in his UFC bouts. His nickname "Mr. Highlight" reflects his ability to deliver spectacular moments in the octagon.
